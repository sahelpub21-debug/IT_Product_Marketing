FAQ

Honest answers to common objections

Have you marketed a SaaS product before?

Not under the formal PMM title. I’ve operated product-adjacent: positioning, GTM briefs, in-app messaging and enablement. Public artifacts (HPS excerpts, Fan4All case) show method & impact.

What about pricing/packaging?

I contribute with customer voice & competitive inputs; work with Product to inform the decision and the narrative around it.

Lifecycle & retention?

I provide an activation sketch (emails + in-app nudges) aligned to analytics and CS. We iterate post-launch.

How do we measure success?

Demo requests, SQL rate, time-to-first-value, enablement usage by sales, and content-to-pipeline attribution.
