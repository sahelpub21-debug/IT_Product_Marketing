User Journey & UX

From first hook to repeat use — where we remove friction

DiscoverRight audience, clear promise, credibility.
EvaluateProof, demos, transparent policies, compare alternatives.
CommitShort checkout / sign-up, no surprises, guidance.
ActivateOnboarding emails & in-app nudges to Time-to-First-Value.
ReturnReminders, new use cases, community & support.
What I inspect
  • Message clarity vs. jobs-to-be-done
  • Number of steps & copy friction
  • Trust signals & risk removal (refunds, SLA, security)
  • Routing from content to product pages (PDP/demo)
What I propose
  • Remove/merge steps, reposition key actions
  • Micro-copy & FAQs for objections
  • Feature suggestion or partner/API opportunities when relevant
  • Experiments with clear success criteria
